Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled in the القرآن الكريم (Quran) mobile application (the “App”).

1. No Data Collection

We respect your privacy. The App is designed to function entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or device identifiers to us or any third party.

2. Local Storage (Bookmarks)

The App allows users to save bookmarks to track reading progress.

  • How it works: Bookmark data is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences.
  • Your control: This data never leaves your device. You may remove it at any time by clearing the App’s data/cache in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

3. Permissions

The App does not require sensitive permissions such as Location, Camera, Contacts, or Microphone access. It only uses standard access necessary to display Quranic content stored within the application.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or cloud-based data collection services.

5. Children’s Privacy

Since the App does not collect any personal information, it is suitable for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any future changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
